EXCEL FORMULAS TO CONVERT:
--------------------------
Key formulas from TrueStrokes_Engine_V3.xlsx:

1. Handicap Differential:
   = (Score - Rating) × (113 / Slope)
   
2. Estimated Handicap:
   = AVERAGE of best 8 differentials from last 10 rounds
   
3. Normalized Stats:
   - FW% = (Total FW Hit / Total FW Available)
   - GIR% = (Total GIR / Total Holes)
   - Putts/GIR = (Total Putts on GIR / Total GIR)
   - Scramble% = (Total Scrambles / Total Missed Greens)
   
4. Tier Assignment:
   Based on Estimated Handicap ranges:
   - 100+ Golfer: 27+ HCP
   - 90s Golfer: 21 HCP
   - Mid 80s: 14.5 HCP
   - High Single Digit: 9 HCP
   - Single Digit: 5.5 HCP
   - Low Handicap: 3.5 HCP
   - Scratch: 1 HCP
   - Plus: -1 HCP

5. Strokes Gained:
   Complex formulas from Strokes_Gained sheet
   Compares user stats to tier baselines
   Distributes gap across 4 categories
